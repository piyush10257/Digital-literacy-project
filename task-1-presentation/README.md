# Task 1: Digital Literacy Awareness Infographic

## Project Overview
For this task, I acted as a Student Digital Ambassador to create a one-page visual resource. This infographic helps my batchmates understand the core pillars of digital literacy.

## Tool Used
I used **Canva** to design this infographic because of its professional templates and intuitive design tools.

## Content Focus
My design covers:
1. What Digital Literacy is.
2. Useful digital tools for students (Google Drive, Canva).
3. Safe internet practices (MFA and strong passwords).

## Reflection
I found it interesting how icons can simplify complex topics. The most difficult part was choosing only the most important information to keep the design clean.
